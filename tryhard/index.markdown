**Tryhard** is an upcoming sports RPG about managing an underdog rugby club in [New Zealand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Zealand).
- Outsmart opponents and upgrade your squad
- But also feed your dog and go on dates
- In this epic saga of love, betrayal, and community sports.
