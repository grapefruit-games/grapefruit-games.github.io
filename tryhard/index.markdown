# TRYHARD

**Tryhard** is an upcoming sports RPG about managing an underdog rugby club in <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Zealand/" target="_blank">New Zealand</a>.

- Outsmart opponents and upgrade your squad!
- Manage a failing club, send emails, argue with the bank - the possibilities are endless!
- Pay the rent, feed your dog, and go on dates. <i>Can you have it all?</i>
- Experience an epic saga of love, betrayal, and community sports.
